About Us

Mawingu is a web application that focuses on weather forecasting around the globe. We leverage on modern technologies to make accurate weather predictions.We have been in existence for two years now.We pride ourselves having been recognized by NASA as the most reliable reliable weather company in the sub saharan region.

Partners
